Our Process – “Plan, Build, Ship” in Monthly Iterations

We follow a simple yet effective process to deliver continuous value to your business, iterating monthly so you see steady progress without long wait times:

Plan: We kick off each cycle with a focused planning session. In a quick discovery meeting, we identify your biggest pain points and opportunities. Together, we set clear Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) with measurable KPIs, so we know exactly what success looks like for the next iteration.
Build: Our team gets to work implementing the solution. Whether we’re configuring an automation bot, designing a new interface, or developing a custom feature, we execute the tasks using the best tools available (including AI assistance or low-code platforms when suitable). Throughout this stage, we handle all the details – you won’t be stuck in status meetings or micromanaging tasks.
Ship: At the end of the month, we deliver the completed improvements and integrate them into your business. We review the results against the OKRs we set, confirm the KPIs are met, and gather your feedback. Then it’s straight into the next cycle, using what we learned to plan the following month’s tasks. This iterative approach ensures you get tangible results quickly and consistently, with the flexibility to adjust as we go.

Why Our Pricing Model Works

Our subscription-based approach eliminates the traditional pain points of project-based pricing, giving you predictable costs and unlimited flexibility.

No Hourly Bills

Forget about tracking hours or surprise invoices. You pay a flat monthly rate based on how many tasks we can work on at the same time (for example, 1 task at a time, 2 tasks at a time, etc.).

Unlimited Requests

There's no limit to the number of ideas, improvements, or bug fixes you can propose. Simply add them to your backlog. We will plan and execute them one by one (or several at once, depending on your subscription) in our ongoing monthly cycles.

Adjust as Needed

Our subscription model is month-to-month. Need to speed up development? Increase the number of parallel tasks. Need to pause or slow down? You can scale back – no long-term contracts locking you in. It's flexibility that grows with your business.
